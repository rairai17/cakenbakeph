RESERVATIONS

3. Once order is sent, changing of pick up date and time is subject for approval. Changes in pick up date and time will be allowed with a rescheduling/inconvenience fee of P150 for bento cakes, P200 for regular and P300 for two-tier cakes.

4. Changing of reserved order is not allowed. Reservation fees are non-refundable.

5. No changes in cake flavor and design (including dedication) once final cake design and final payment are confirmed.

6. NO JOY RESERVERS. Joy reservers are automatically blocked from ordering in the future.

7. No pasingits/singit. We will not accept orders/reservations for fully booked dates via Instagram, Facebook, and Email.

2. Neither party shall be responsible for events beyond its reasonable control, such as acts of God, weather delays, or other fortuitous events. If pick up is postponed due to inclement weather or other conditions beyond CAKE N BAKE's control, your order may be rescheduled for another time.

3. In case of cancellation by CAKE N BAKE, we will offer rescheduling or full refund of your order.

1. We only accept payments via GCASH and BPI.

2. Strictly no Cash on Delivery (COD).

3. Non-payment of orders will refrain you from ordering in the future.

SHIPPING / PICK-UP

1. Our pick up time is STRICTLY from 11AM until 4PM TUESDAY - SUNDAY only. You are not allowed to pick up your order before your schedule. If you need your order earlier than our available pick up time, please book your order a day ahead. Pick up details will be sent via email minutes before your scheduled time. If you fail to give us a specific pick up time, your order will be scheduled for our last batch of the day - afternoon pick up.

2. We DO NOT schedule/book delivery for our customers. You may book your order pick-up via couriers such as Lalamove, Grab, Transportify, Angkas, etc.

3. We recommend that our regular-sized Custom Cakes (6” and bigger) be transported via car/MPV with flat surface and air conditioning, while our Bento cakes can be transported via motorcycle with insulated box.

4. CAKE N BAKE will NOT be liable for any damages while in transit as we make sure that our products are made in the best condition.

5. If you fail to pick up your cake on the day of your booking, you may still pick up your order the next business day/s (up to three (3) days only) with a storage fee of 50 (for bento/small cakes) & 100 (for regular/big cakes) per night per cake. Failure to pick up after three (3) days, CAKE N BAKE will reserve the right to dispose your order.

PRODUCTS

1. We do not customize cake flavors. You may see available sizes and flavors in our Order and Menu pages. For custom sizes, kindly email us for inquiries.

2. For custom cakes, we try our best to execute customers’ request; however, we cannot guarantee that we can exactly copy your design/peg as we have our own style.

3. For paintings, themed cakes, and designs coming from the internet/other shops, CAKE N BAKE has the discretion to interpret and to tweak design according to our own style.

4. Please double check your cake design form. We do not allow changes in design once it’s confirmed. This is to avoid confusion during the cake designing process.

5. We do not make cake design drafts. We simply refer to the brief and artists’ interpretation. If you miss to put a detail (example: base color, font style), this will be up to our artists’ discretion. Rest assured that we will put what we think is best and up to CAKE N BAKE’s standards.

6. Our custom cakes are made with Swiss Meringue Buttercream. Cakes with 3D design and two tier may have dowels / sticks / straws in them. We do not use other mediums such as fondant, sugar/gum paste, molding chocolates, etc.

7. Cake Storage: All of our cakes MUST be refrigerated as soon as you receive it. Our cakes can stand for up to 1-1.5 hours while in transit. Shelf-life for buttercream cakes can be good for up to 5 days, if stored properly in the fridge.

8. Allergy Warning: All of our products are made with shared equipment that may have come in contact with ingredients containing allergens including but not limited to: Nuts, Dairy, Eggs, Gluten, etc. CAKE N BAKE will not be liable for any allergic reactions coming from the said ingredients.
