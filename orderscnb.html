This is a reservation for custom design of CAKE N BAKE cake. There is no limit to the slots that you can reserve as long as the dates that you want are still available.

Cake design restrictions:

We only use Swiss Meringue Buttercream and/or American Buttercream, depending on the design. We do not use other mediums such as fondant, sugar/gum paste, molding chocolates, etc.

We do not do Edible Floral cakes, Fruit Topper, Spotify codes, Drip cakes, and Money cakes.

FOR CAKE DESIGN REQUIREMENTS AND PRICING, PLEASE INQUIRE VIA EMAIL OR INSTAGRAM DM @_cakenbake.

Please take note of the following:

THIS IS ONLY THE RESERVATION FEE FOR YOUR CAKE. The Cake N Bake team will reach out to you via email within 24-48 business hours for the cake design form after reservation fee is settled. Final cake price will be based on your final cake design.

You have 24 hours to pay for your reservation fee. Failure to pay on time means your reservation slot will be voided. Please reply your proof of payment to the confirmation email. Order will be cancelled if there is no proof of payment.

Changes in pickup date and time is subject for approval. (Please see Reservations under our Terms & Conditions for changes)

HOW TO ORDER FROM CAKE N BAKE

How to Order

STEP 1

ORDER FROM OUR WEBSITE

www.cakenbake.com

View all the products and select the product you want.

Select your pick up date and time of the product of your choice.

Please read our Terms & Conditions before checking out!

STEP 2

PAY FOR YOUR RESERVATION FEE

After check out, you will receive an automated email confirmation

*If you forgot our payment details, just click on the VIEW ORDER button located in the email confirmation*

Send your proof of payment by replying to the email confirmation

Kindly wait for the Cake N Bake team to confirm your reservation fee

STEP 3

CUSTOMIZE YOUR DESIRED CAKE

Send us the required details and fill out the design form

When everything is finalized, you will receive an email regarding the final payment

Pay as you did on STEP 2!

THEN WE ARE DONE!

Final payment confirmation will be sent and next update will be sent via email on the day of cake pick-up
